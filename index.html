Merry Christmas.

Or just… hello.

If you’re here tonight, maybe the world feels quieter than it should. Maybe heavier.

That’s okay.

You don’t have to be joyful. You don’t have to celebrate. You don’t have to pretend this day feels good.

Being alone doesn’t mean you’re forgotten. Feeling low doesn’t mean you’re weak.

Somewhere, someone made this hoping it would reach you — not to fix anything, just to sit with you for a moment.

Thank you for staying.
I’m glad you’re here.

Wishing you a little warmth tonight.
You are not alone.
